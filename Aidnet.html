About Network AID

Network AID is a program designed to provide financial assistance to subscribers of participating network providers during times of need. Our mission is to support our community members when they face unexpected financial challenges.

How It Works

Subscribers can apply for AID by completing a simple application process that includes identity verification. Once approved, funds are deposited directly into the user's account and can be withdrawn according to the program guidelines.

Eligibility

To be eligible for Network AID, you must be an active subscriber of a participating network provider and meet the program requirements. Each application is reviewed individually based on the provided information.
